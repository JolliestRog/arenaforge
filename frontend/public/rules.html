Rules

Simple rules for a simple tool.

Use it in good faith

DeckForge exists to help players build decks. Use it for that purpose. Don't use it to cheat, harass, or gain an unfair advantage in organized play beyond what the tool is designed to provide.

Don't abuse the service

  • No automated scraping or bot traffic.
  • No attempting to overwhelm the server.
  • No probing for vulnerabilities or attempting unauthorized access.

Deck suggestions are a starting point

DeckForge generates suggestions based on a heuristic algorithm with a curated card pool. The output is a starting point, not gospel. Adapt it to your playstyle and the specific meta you're playing in.

Respect the game

MTG Arena has its own Terms of Service. DeckForge generates legal decklists — what you do with them is your responsibility.
