Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026  ·  Unofficial fan tool, not affiliated with Wizards of the Coast.

The short version

DeckForge does not collect personal information. Your collection data is sent to our server only to generate your deck, and is never stored. You can optionally save your collection in your own browser for convenience.

What we collect

  • Server logs — standard web server access logs (IP address, timestamp, request path). Rotated regularly. Not linked to any personal identity.

What we do not collect

  • Your MTG Arena collection (not stored on our server)
  • Your deck lists or build choices
  • Name, email, or account information of any kind
  • Device fingerprints or tracking cookies
  • Any analytics or third-party tracking

How deck generation works

When you generate a deck, your collection is sent to our server to run the deck optimizer. It is used only to process your request and is not written to any database or retained after your request completes.

If you choose to save your collection between sessions, it is stored in your browser's localStorage — on your device only, never transmitted to us. You can clear it at any time using the "Clear saved collection" button on the import screen, or by clearing your browser's site data.

Third-party services

  • Hosting — the site runs on a self-hosted server. No third-party analytics, ad networks, or tracking pixels.
  • Cloudflare — DNS and TLS termination. Standard CDN access logs apply.
  • Scryfall — card images are loaded directly from Scryfall's CDN when you hover a card. Scryfall's own API policies apply to those requests.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, the date above will be updated.
